Security Notes

          

            
    
              
  • 
                Your channel wallet is stored in your local workspace at
                .
              
    • 
              
  • 
                Even if you lose the channel wallet file, only your Ethereum private key, together with the correct
                channel context, can recover your channel wallet as long as you still know the wallet password.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                If your channel wallet file and wallet password are both stolen, your channel funds can be stolen.
              
    • 
              
  • 
                If you lose your wallet password, you lose the ability to derive your channel L2 private key. In that
                case, you lose ownership of all notes because you can no longer use them, and that ownership cannot be
                recovered.

Command Guide

          

            
    
              
  1. 
                Join a channel: join-channel
                
    
                  join-channel --channel-name 'my-private-channel' --password '__WALLET_PASSWORD__' --network 'sepolia' --private-key '__L1_PRIVATE_KEY__' --alchemy-api-key '__ALCHEMY_API_KEY__'
                
    
              
    2. 
              
  2. 
                Create notes: deposit-bridge -> deposit-channel -> mint-notes
                
    
                  deposit-bridge --amount '100' --network 'sepolia' --private-key '__L1_PRIVATE_KEY__' --alchemy-api-key '__ALCHEMY_API_KEY__'
    
                  deposit-channel --wallet 'my-private-channel-0xYourL1Address' --password '__WALLET_PASSWORD__' --network 'sepolia' --amount '100'
    
                  mint-notes --wallet 'my-private-channel-0xYourL1Address' --password '__WALLET_PASSWORD__' --network 'sepolia' --amounts '["50","50","0"]'
                
    
              
    3. 
              
  3. 
                Split, merge, or transfer note ownership: transfer-notes
                
    
                  transfer-notes --wallet 'my-private-channel-0xYourL1Address' --password '__WALLET_PASSWORD__' --network 'sepolia' --note-ids '["0xNoteIdA"]' --recipients '["0xRecipientL2AddressA","0xRecipientL2AddressB"]' --amounts '["25","25"]'
                
    
              
    4. 
              
  4. 
                Redeem notes back to L1: redeem-notes -> withdraw-channel -> withdraw-bridge
                
    
                  redeem-notes --wallet 'my-private-channel-0xYourL1Address' --password '__WALLET_PASSWORD__' --network 'sepolia' --note-ids '["0xNoteIdA"]'
    
                  withdraw-channel --wallet 'my-private-channel-0xYourL1Address' --password '__WALLET_PASSWORD__' --network 'sepolia' --amount '25'
    
                  withdraw-bridge --amount '25' --network 'sepolia' --private-key '__L1_PRIVATE_KEY__' --alchemy-api-key '__ALCHEMY_API_KEY__'
